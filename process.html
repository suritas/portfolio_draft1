The following is from a consulting project with CNBC

HOW CAN USER BEHAVIORS SURROUNDING FINANCE AND VIDEO APPS
INFLUENCE GESTURES AND ARCHITECTURE FOR THE CNBC IOS REDESIGN OF MARKETS AND LIVE TV?


1. BACKGROUND RESEARCH
Initial scoping session with Emerging Platforms Team Reps.
Brainstorm of competing applications
Identification of current (35+ passive investor), and target/newer audience (20+ aspirational professional)

2. HYPOTHESIS
Content & Markets info exclusivity, as well as familiar gestures for video content will lead to user retention.

3. INVESTIGATION PLAN
Interaction Map (familiarize pain points)
User Interviews (video app & cable TV use)
Competitive Analysis (compare competing apps mentioned by users)
Survey & analysis (to contextualize interview results)
Usability Study/Observations

4. USER INTERVIEW & OBSERVATION
Screen Interviewees: professionals, mobile video content viewers, some markets interest ,include users from age brackets of interest to our client (18-22, 22-34, and 34+)
Separate out usability-study questions (which are task oriented) from initial user interview questions (which can be used towards personas & comparative evaluation)
Conduct User Interviews, take audio recordings & transcript/notes
Individual prep for group interpretation session of survey results
